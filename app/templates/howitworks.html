In the world of Pokemon, trainers like yourself with battle each other using their strongest Pokemon partners. There are many ways that a battle can be decided.

  • The type of your Pokemon can play a big role in the advantage one Pokemon has over another. For examle, a water type Pokemon is strong against a fire type, but that water Pokemon will be weak against electric attacks.
  • The level of your partner Pokemon shows its experience level and overall strength. A level 40 Pokemon will generally be much stronger than a Pokemon that is only at level 25.
  • Another main factor to determine which of two Pokemon are stronger is comparing their Stats. Each Pokemon has its own unique stats for attack, defense, health points (HP), and speed.
